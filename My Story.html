My Story

They say when you do what you love, you never work a day in your life. Some of my foundest memories as a child always entailed me pulling apart anything I could get my hands on and reassembling it. This passion for building transgressed to me persuing a degree in Mechanical Engineering at the University of Maryland. I was always good with numbers and also recieved a dual degree in Economics. The journey wasn't easy but I've always been determined to make the most out of every opportunity. Shortly after completing my bachelor degrees, I started working at a solar company as an Analyst. This role allowed me to enhance my leadership skills while managing and assigning technians to various solar projects. Even though I was excelling in my role, I knew that I needed to further my education. I applied to Bowie State Universtity, and was excepted in their Computer Sciecnce Graduate School.

No one could have predicted the tragedy of dealing with a pandemic but with less than a year away from completing my degree I look forward to my future endeavours in the field of Software Engineering.
